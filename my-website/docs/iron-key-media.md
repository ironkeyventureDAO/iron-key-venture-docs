## üëãüèº Introduction

With the ever-growing popularity and influence of the cryptocurrency and blockchain industry, there is a tremendous opportunity to establish a strong media presence in this space. This proposal aims to leverage diverse media outlets to cover the latest developments, provide in-depth analysis, educate beginners, share opinions, and create engaging content for crypto and venture enthusiasts. Specifically, it addresses the opportunity to scale and promote the Iron Key ecosystem.

By delivering informative and valuable content, we can establish ourselves as a trusted source of information and analysis within the web3 venture community.

Monetization opportunities for this media proposal include sponsored content, advertising partnerships, affiliate marketing, premium subscriptions for exclusive content (access to deal flow), and collaborations with other blockchain projects. By strategically monetizing our media efforts, we can ensure the sustainability and growth of our media platform while continuing to deliver high-quality content to our audience.

By seizing this opportunity and utilizing various media platforms, we can contribute to the dissemination of knowledge, foster discussions, and promote the growth and adoption of cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology.

## Iron Key Media One-Pager

**Overview**

At Iron Key, we strive to offer premium venture and angel investor content from the premier minds in the industry by hosting AMAs (Ask Me Anything) and Twitter Spaces. These events are designed to engage your audience, enhance brand visibility, and create meaningful interactions. We provide various options tailored to your preferences, ensuring a "red carpet" experience.*

---

**Schedule & Availability**

Day Options:
- Thursday: 10:00 AM - 1 PM; 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM EDT
- Friday: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM; 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM EDT

Duration:
- Each session is capped at 1 hour.

---

**Event Formats**

1. **Twitter Spaces**
   - Type: Audio only
   - Platform: Twitter
   - Features: Live audience interaction, real-time Q&A, recording option for later playback.
   - Included: Personalized introductory messages, branded promotional graphics, and post-event highlights.

2. **Live Stream Via Twitter & LinkedIn Live**
   - Type: Video
   - Platform: Twitter & LinkedIn; streamed through Streamyard
   - Features: Professional network reach, video interaction, audience comments, and Q&A.
   - Included: Customized event page, premium promotional banners, and exclusive networking opportunities post-event.

3. **Discord Stage**
   - Type: Video
   - Platform: Discord
   - Features: Interactive voice chat, community engagement, and moderated discussions.
   - Included: VIP voice channels, custom emojis and badges for participants, and exclusive post-event summaries. This will be a smaller and more intimate setting in a gated channel, but it is a curated audience of the most involved members in the Iron Key ecosystem.

4. **Pre-recorded AMA**
   - Type: Video
   - Platform: Youtube
   - Features: Planned and edited content, longer form opportunity, distraction free from community questions
   - Included: Recording link to share with community, custom branded banners; CTA links included at the end of interview

---

**Enhancements for an Optimized Event Experience**

- Branded Invitations: Custom-designed digital invitations for your audience.
- Exclusive Content: Behind-the-scenes access, sneak peeks of upcoming releases, and special announcements.
- Premium Support: Dedicated support team for technical assistance and event coordination.
- Post-Event Analytics: Comprehensive reports on audience engagement, feedback, and reach.

---

**Booking & Coordination**

To schedule your interview, please visit our [Event Booking Calendar](./event-booking). We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance to ensure optimal preparation and promotion. We will contact you with next steps through your preferred medium - Telegram, email, or Discord.

We look forward to creating an exceptional experience for you and your audience.

**Note: All events will be conducted in English*.
