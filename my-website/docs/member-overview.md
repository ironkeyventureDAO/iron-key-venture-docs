## Culture of Meetings

Maintaining effective meeting standards is vital for project momentum as a fully virtual organization. We communicate often to ensure all team members are on the same page and aware of individual next steps. Despite the intensity of our work, we promote a relaxed meeting atmosphere in which people feel comfortable and eager to collaborate.

---

:::tip Meeting Etiquette Guidelines

‚öñÔ∏è Please follow these guidelines for meeting etiquette:

This is a server open to EVERYONE seeking to learn about venture; however, all of our deals are contained in gated channels to ensure that Venture Fellows and Partners are free to discuss all deals in confidence with committed investors and partners.
